General Questions

What services does Targetmnc provide?
Targetmnc offers a comprehensive range of business consultancy services including HR solutions, staffing services, payroll management, training and development, and business process outsourcing. We help businesses optimize their operations and human resources.
How long has Targetmnc been in business?
We have been serving clients since 2010, with over a decade of experience in providing top-notch consultancy services to businesses of all sizes across various industries.
Where is your company located?
Our headquarters are located at 702, Midtown Business Park, Sector 20, Haryana. We serve clients both locally and internationally.

HR & Staffing Services

What types of staffing solutions do you offer?
We provide temporary staffing, permanent placement, contract staffing, and executive search services tailored to meet your specific business needs and industry requirements.
How do you ensure the quality of candidates?
All candidates go through a rigorous screening process that includes resume verification, skill assessments, background checks, and multiple rounds of interviews to ensure they meet our high standards and your specific requirements.
Can you help with bulk hiring requirements?
Absolutely. We specialize in bulk recruitment and have successfully managed large-scale hiring projects for various industries. Our team can design a customized hiring strategy to meet your volume requirements efficiently.

Payroll & Compliance

Do you handle payroll processing for clients?
Yes, we offer comprehensive payroll management services including salary processing, tax deductions, PF/ESI compliance, and generating all necessary reports and payslips.
How do you ensure compliance with labor laws?
Our team of legal and HR experts stays updated with all labor law amendments. We conduct regular audits and implement systems to ensure full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
Can you help with statutory registrations?
Yes, we assist with all necessary statutory registrations including PF, ESI, PT, GST, Shop & Establishment registration, and other compliance requirements based on your business needs.

Training & Development

What kind of training programs do you offer?
We provide a wide range of training programs including leadership development, technical skills training, soft skills enhancement, compliance training, and customized programs designed for your specific organizational needs.
Can training be customized for our industry?
Absolutely. All our training programs can be tailored to address your industry-specific challenges and requirements. We work closely with you to understand your needs before designing the training curriculum.
Do you provide online training options?
Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual training options to accommodate your team's needs and preferences. Our virtual training programs are interactive and designed to be as effective as in-person sessions.
