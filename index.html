Our Journey

Three Decades of Excellence

From our humble beginnings in Nagpur to becoming a pan-India leader

1995-2000

Humble Beginnings

Founded in Nagpur with just 5 employees, we started as a local recruitment agency serving Maharashtra region.

2000-2005

Regional Expansion

Expanded our services to neighboring states, establishing offices in Mumbai and Hyderabad.

2005-2010

Expanded utside India

Implemented our first digital recruitment platform, revolutionizing how we connect candidates with employers.

2010-2015

National Recognition

Established offices in Delhi, Bangalore, and Kolkata, becoming a truly national recruitment partner.

2020-2025

Award-Winning Services

Recognized as "Best Recruitment Agency" by National HR Association for three consecutive years.

2016-2020

Specialization Era

Launched dedicated verticals for IT, Healthcare, and Engineering recruitment with industry-specific experts.

2021-Present

Pan-India Leadership

Now serving clients across 25+ cities with 150+ recruitment specialists and 50,000+ successful placements.
